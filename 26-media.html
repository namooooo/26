Maharashtra reported 44,388 new COVID cases, 15,351 recoveries, and 12 deaths today. The capital Mumbai also logged 19,474 Covid cases, a marginal dip of 4% from yesterday. Amid a fresh spurt of Covid cases in Uttar Pradesh, the state government on Sunday ordered closure of all schools till January 16 and said the night curfew will continue to be in force between 10 pm and 6 am in the state. The government, in a statement said, the night curfew, which happened to be in force between 11 pm and 6 am since December 25 had been extended by two hours on Tuesday.
